Partner program

Refer clients. Get credit on confirmed bookings.

For event coordinators, hosts, photographers, and suppliers in Lipa & Batangas. Share your tracked link — when a referred customer books and completes an event, you earn referral credit we apply to future rentals or agreed payouts.

How it works

  • Request your partner code on Messenger (we assign a short code like maria-events).
  • Share your link on Facebook, Viber, or in proposals — clicks and inquiries are tagged automatically.
  • When the booking is confirmed and the event is completed, referral credit is recorded to your partner account.

Build your referral link

Enter the partner code we gave you. Share the homepage link — attribution stays for the whole session.

Sample: https://easyrentalph.vercel.app/?ref=your-code&utm_source=partner&utm_medium=referral

Apply or ask questions

Message us with your name, business type, and typical event sizes. We reply on Messenger with your code and program terms.
