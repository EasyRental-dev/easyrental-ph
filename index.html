Delivery

How delivery is calculated

Delivery fees are not flat rates on this website. Each booking is priced in our app from your exact venue, distance, and logistics—then we send the confirmed total and schedule on Messenger.

What our booking app uses

  • Distance and route from our base to your venue
  • Vehicle and dispatch for your event date and time window
  • Item volume and loading complexity
  • Setup, takedown, pickup, and access at the venue

Why we don't list delivery prices here: the same barangay can price differently by address, schedule, and load. Publishing fixed town rates would be misleading. Send your complete venue address and event details—we run the calculation and reply with your exact delivery fee.

What you'll see on your quote

Package and item rates on this site are equipment only. Your Messenger quote shows equipment plus the app-calculated delivery (and setup if requested) before you confirm with a deposit. See booking policies.
